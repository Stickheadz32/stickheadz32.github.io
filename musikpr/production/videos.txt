ChipTest1

A quick techno chiptune I made with ModPlug Tracker in 20-30 minutes while at the  RealLan 2016 computer festival in Realgymnasiet during the first hours (GMT+1) of March 26th 2016. Inspiration from Skaven, Alexander Brandon, FantomenK, Michiel Van Den Bos, Kraftwerk, among other artists I forgot to mention.
